Campus Life

Mathematics laboratory

different activities for learning Math

Math Lab serves as a platform to explain abstract concepts that are generally difficult to teach students in the classroom. With the laboratory experience, students will be able to translate what they have read in their texts to practical realities, thereby enhancing their understanding of the learnt concepts and allow them to explore it beyond. Equipped with over 750 components and activities related to Mathematics it is built on a philosophy of “learning by discovery.”
